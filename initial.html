Hello Users, Welcome to ampm jobs portal, this portal is a part time jobs platform - bridge connecting Students and Employers This is a very user friendly application It takes just 2 minutes to complete the registration process
The Bridge helps students and employers connect and select each other depending upon their requirements
The eligible students should be above 18+ years

We help students make some earning while they study for their better living and also become self sustained for their minimum needs. Students will have access to more part time job opportunities in multiple locations and domains of their choice, as we are building on the International concept of “EARN WHILE YOU LEARN” across PAN India This portal allows students to register online and search for part time jobs of their interest as all the vacancies available in our data base or listed based on the guide lines of registration

The employers can post their requirements of part time jobs on the portal for a stipulated time, and also search for students of their choice and line of activities.

Our World Class ecosystem provides a new approach both for students and employers to build an upcoming work force
